Frequently Asked Questions

1. What materials are your desk lamps made of?

Our desk lamps are made of high-quality materials such as metal, plastic, and wood, ensuring durability and style.

2. Do you offer warranties on your products?

Yes, we offer a 1-year warranty on all our desk lamps. Extended warranty options are also available.

3. Can I customize the lamp's color or design?

We provide customization options on select models. Contact our support team for more details.

4. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location.

5. How can I clean and maintain my desk lamp?

For maintenance, use a soft, damp cloth to clean your lamp. Avoid harsh chemicals to protect the finish.
